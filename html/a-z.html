Abnormal loads

Description
An "abnormal load" can generally be described as a vehicle having one or more of the following characteristics - total gross vehicle weight of 40 tonnes or over, an Axle load of more than 11.5 tonnes, a vehicle or its load more than 2.90 metres wide or a vehicle or its load more than 18.65 metres long
