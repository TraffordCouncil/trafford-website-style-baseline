Volunteers Week – Citizens Advice Trafford 75 year celebration

Date
01/06/2014
Location:
Sale
Description
Volunteers' Week is an annual event which takes place on 1-7 June. It celebrates the contribution made by millions of volunteers across the UK. It's run by NCVO in partnership with Volunteer Development Scotland, Volunteer Now (Northern Ireland) and Wales Council for Voluntary Action.From showcasing the different volunteering roles on offer, taster sessions and team challenges with new partners, to volunteer recruitment events, awards ceremonies and launching new volunteering campaigns, events take place throughout the country.Everyone is invited to join this national celebration of volunteers and volunteering, it's up to you to decide exactly how you join in.

The Big Trafford Lunch

Date
01/06/2014
Location:
Across Trafford
Description
We all love a party, so get your friends and neighbours together and let's celebrate the contribution your street makes to Trafford being a great place to live. Hundreds of residents came together for the Jubilee and Olympics, and many of you have carried on partying every year, often for the Big Lunch. This year we want you to make it bigger and better, recapturing that spirit of summer 2012. On 1st June 2014 we want you to hold a Big Trafford Lunch, a day of street parties across Trafford You could have a Trafford themed party, dressing up as characters from our local areas such as Trafford Park or Dunham Massey, have a sporting theme celebrating Trafford's rich sporting heritage, and lots not forget the people that put Trafford on the map in the last 40 years, such as Morrissey, Sir Alex Ferguson and Karl Pilkington!For more information on holding a Big Trafford Lunch street party click here (www.thebiglunch.com )The Council will support you with your Big Trafford Lunch on 1st June 2014 by: Providing Traffic Regulation Orders (TROs) free of charge (road closures) Paying the cost of public liability insurance Procuring professional road closure equipment.This is only available on 1st June 2014, and you must submit an application form by Friday 16th May .
