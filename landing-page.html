Four4adoption|

Four4adoption is an adoption collaboration between four local authorities; Trafford, Cheshire East, Stockport & Tameside.

AdOpt

AdOpt is a parenting programme designed to support adoptive families in the Four4adoption collaboration.

The programme is designed for adopters with children aged 3-8 years, within the first two years of placement.
